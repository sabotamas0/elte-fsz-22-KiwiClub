Welcome to Kiwiclub webapp!

Always wanted to have a kiwi, but you never can't get any? Now here is the KiwiClub, where you can have, keep and raise your own kiwi.

It's important to feed and water a kiwi, otherwise it's going to die, very fast. And you don't want to let your kiwi die, because it would be very sad.

But it's not enough to keep them alive, you can teach them awesome tricks too.

If you got the spirit to have a kiwi, than you can make yours after a fast registration, or if you are already registered, than just sign in.
